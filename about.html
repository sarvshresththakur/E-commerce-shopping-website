Who We Are?

India as a diverse land has a lot of colorful trends when it comes to textiles and apparel. Of course, the collapse of all hype at the dawn of the twentieth century brought a more sensible environment and a more solid view of the fashion industry. As far as we know, the Indian Textile industry is a significant contributor to the country's economy. The industry is providing one of the most fundamental needs of the community and has importance; preserving ongoing development for raising the quality of life. It has established itself as a self-sufficient industry that adds value at every stage of the supply chain right from the production of raw materials to the delivery of finished goods.
